  • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automated attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry.
    2. Economics Have you ever thought that attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, bubby punching, absenteeism, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment.
    3. Produtivity / Efficiency Monitoring and managing attendance manually can be a time-consuming, laborious, and expensive affair. It takes time to process paper sheets and time card, create schedules, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually.
